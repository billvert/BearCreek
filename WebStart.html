Getting started on the Web

The internet is obviously an incredibly powerful and popular way of connecting with existing and potential customers. Fortunately, it's easy to set up your website with your own domain name. We can help every step of the way.

Web hosting


Start by finding a web host. These companies will allow you to search for an available domain name that matches your company's name. This domain name will be the address of your website and your email address if you choose to add that.

Build the web site


You have a number of options when building your web site. You can hire someone to build your site (we do this); you can use a Content Management System (CMS) to manage your site; or you can learn HTML and construct the web page(s) by yourself.

Make sure people find your web site

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) consists of a variety of methods that will allow your website to rank higher in search engine returns. There are many techniques to enhance your rankings, and companies are available to help with this.

Monitor how often people are viewing your web site

Google Analytics is a free service that will show you how many people are viewing your site, where they are located, and what keywords they searched to reach you.
